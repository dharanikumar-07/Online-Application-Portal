User Access:
Students: Individuals who need to apply for leave access the portal to submit their leave requests.
Submission of Leave Requests:
Users log in to the portal and fill out a leave request form. This form typically includes details such as the type of leave , the duration of the leave, and any supporting documentation if required.
Workflow and Approval:
The portal often incorporates a workflow for leave approval. After submission, the leave request is routed to the relevant authorities or tutors for approval. Approvers can review the leave request, check for conflicts, and decide whether to approve or decline the application.
Communication:
The portal facilitates communication between the applicant and the approver. Notifications and alerts are often sent to both parties to keep them informed about the status of the leave application.
Categories:
In this application portal,the leave application is divided into two types namely,casual and medical
User Access:
Students who are assigned on-duty tasks access the portal to submit their on-duty applications.
Submission of On-duty Requests:
Student log in to the portal and fill out an on-duty request form. This form typically includes details such as the nature of the on-duty assignment, the date and time, and any other relevant information.
Workflow and Approval:
The portal incorporates a workflow for on-duty assignment approval. After submission, the on-duty request is routed to the relevant authorities or tutors for approval. Approvers review the request, ensuring that it aligns with organizational needs and staffing requirements, before deciding whether to approve or decline the on-duty application.
Communication:
The portal facilitates communication between the applicant and the approver. Notifications and alerts are often sent to both parties to keep them informed about the status of the on-duty application.
Categories:
In this application portal,the Onduty application is divided into two types namely,Internal and Casual

Application Details:
